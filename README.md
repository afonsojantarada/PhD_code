#PhD  Code

Core set of scripts developed during the first year of my PhD. These codes are specifically designed to extract and analyze trajectories corresponding to mass coordinates where the abundances of p-nuclei reach their maximum values.

The workflow implemented in these scripts focuses on identifying and tracking the physical conditions associated with peak p-nuclei production, enabling a detailed investigation of their formation and evolution. By isolating the relevant mass zones and following their temporal behavior, the code supports a deeper understanding of the nucleosynthesis processes and the underlying astrophysical environments.

These tools serve as a foundation for further analysis and model validation throughout the course of my research.
