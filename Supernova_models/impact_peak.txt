In this folder it is possible to evalutate the impact of the maximum value for  p-isotope abundances, comparing the value of X_max with sum X, for M > M_cut. 4 different models will be analysed :
1) M = 15 M_sun | Z = 0.01
2) M = 20 M_sun | Z = 0.01
3) M = 15 M_sun | Z = 0.02
4) M = 20 M_sun | Z = 0.02
